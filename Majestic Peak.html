DESCRIPTION

This abstract painting uses bold, swirling colors to evoke a sense of dynamic movement and intense energy. The artwork contrasts deep blues and bright whites in the upper half, resembling turbulent skies or ocean waves, while fiery reds, oranges, and yellows dominate the lower part, suggesting flames or a molten landscape. This juxtaposition between cool and warm tones creates a dramatic effect, drawing the eye through the contrasting elements and evoking a feeling of chaos meeting calmness.

Serpent’s Illusion: Breaking Through the Layers

$800

Perfect For: Living room, hallway, or dining area

DESCRIPTION

This painting depicts a surreal scene where bright, neon-colored serpentine creatures and spherical objects seem to break through the surface of undulating black-and-white striped layers. The 3D-like effect of the layers creates a dynamic, illusionary background, while the vibrant green and orange elements contrast sharply against the monochromatic waves. The serpents, with their sharp fangs, appear to be emerging from or disappearing into this warped environment, adding a sense of movement and surrealism to the composition.

Medium: Oil on Canvas

Size: 16'x16'

Style: Surrealist

How Imaginerium Theater Started

Azalea Rosziel Lenihan
CEO

I noticed a common challenge that many artists, including myself, were facing: the difficulty in reaching a wider audience and finding a reliable platform to showcase and sell art. The traditional way of selling art online often led to lower sales and more negotiating with middlemen, while sifting through overwhelming options made it hard for buyers who appreciated the work to connect with potential sellers.

My first painting was something I was proud of, but despite my efforts to share it, I couldn't get the attention I hoped for. Local galleries didn't sell it, and I was unsure how to reach a wider audience. I shared the frustration many artists experience. This led me to create a platform that connects artists with people who truly appreciate their art, without barriers.

That's why I created this website: to solve that problem. It's not just a space to display art but also a tool to help artists find their audience and get the recognition they deserve. We aim to make it easier for artists to focus on making beautiful art while we handle the rest. This platform allows artists to connect with their audience, promote their masterpieces, and achieve the recognition they deserve.

As our company has grown, we have been able to get our voice out there!
